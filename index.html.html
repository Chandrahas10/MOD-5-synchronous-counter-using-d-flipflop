This MOD-5 counter uses three D flip-flops (7474) to count from 0 to 4 and then reset to 0. The circuit follows this sequence: 000 ‚Üí 001 ‚Üí 010 ‚Üí 011 ‚Üí 100 ‚Üí 000

Logic Equations:

  • D0 = NOT Q0 (7404)
  • D1 = Q0 XOR Q1 (7486)
  • D2 = Q2 ? 0 : (Q1 AND Q0) (7486, 7408)

Components Used:

  • 3x D Flip-Flops (7474)
  • 1x NOT Gate (7404)
  • 1x AND Gate (7408)
  • 2x XOR Gates (7486)
